## 4. Domain-Adaptive Grammar

- Expert explanation grammar (teaching/explaining complex systems)

## 5. Ultra-Advanced Conditional and Modal Logic
- Multi-step hypothetical reasoning chains
- Counterfactual argument structures across timeframes
- Fine certainty calibration under uncertainty

- Handle pressure interactions with natural fluency
- Adapt grammar instantly by role, power, and audience
- Sound precise, nuanced, and context-appropriate
