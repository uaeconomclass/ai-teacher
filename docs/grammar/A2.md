# A2 Grammar Plan (Speaking First)

## 1. Past and Future Core
- Past Simple regular/irregular verbs
- Past questions and negatives (`did/didn't`)
- Future with `going to` (plans)
- Future with `will` (instant decisions/predictions)

## 2. Time and Aspect Expansion
- Present Continuous for future arrangements
- Present Simple for timetables
- Frequency adverbs refinement (`often, hardly ever`)
- Time clauses with `when, before, after`

## 3. Modal Verbs (Practical Use)
- `can/could` (ability, polite requests)
- `must/mustn't` (rules)
- `have to/don't have to` (obligation)
- `should/shouldn't` (advice)

## 4. Nouns, Quantifiers, Determiners
- Countable vs uncountable refinement
- `much/many, a lot of, few/a few, little/a little`
- `some/any/no` deeper usage
- `one/ones` reference

## 5. Comparison and Description
- Comparatives: `bigger, more useful`
- Superlatives: `the best, the most expensive`
- Equality: `as ... as`
- Basic intensifiers: `very, really, too, enough`

## 6. Pronouns and Reference Control
- Reflexive pronouns: `myself, yourself`
- Possessive pronouns: `mine, yours`
- Relative pronouns (intro): `who, which, that`

## 7. Speaking Connectors
- Sequencing: `first, then, after that, finally`
- Reason/result: `because, so`
- Contrast intro: `but, although` (basic)

## 8. Speaking Outcomes (A2)
- Narrate past events in sequence
- Discuss short-term plans
- Compare options and give simple advice
- Sustain familiar-topic conversation for 5-7 minutes
