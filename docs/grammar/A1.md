# A1 Grammar Plan (Speaking First)

## 1. Sentence Foundations
- Personal pronouns: `I, you, he, she, it, we, they`
- `to be` in present: `am/is/are`
- Basic word order: `Subject + Verb + Object`
- Simple noun phrase: adjective + noun (`a big room`)

## 2. Articles, Nouns, Quantity
- `a/an`, basic `the`
- Singular/plural nouns
- Basic countable/uncountable nouns
- `some/any` (intro)
- `this/that/these/those`

## 3. Core Tenses and Forms
- Present Simple (affirmative)
- Present Simple (negative with `do/does not`)
- Present Simple questions (`Do you...?`)
- Present Continuous for now (`I am working`)
- Imperatives (`Open the book`, `Don't worry`)

## 4. Questions and Short Answers
- `Wh-` questions: `what, where, when, who`
- `yes/no` questions with `be` and `do`
- Short answers: `Yes, I do. / No, she isn't.`

## 5. Location, Existence, Ability
- `there is/there are`
- Basic prepositions: `in, on, at, under, next to`
- `can/can't` for ability and permission

## 6. Possession and Reference
- Possessive adjectives: `my, your, his, her...`
- Possessive `'s`
- Object pronouns: `me, him, her, us, them`

## 7. Connectors for Early Speech
- `and, but, because`
- Basic time markers: `today, now, always, usually`

## 8. Speaking Outcomes (A1)
- Introduce yourself and family
- Ask and answer routine questions
- Describe daily routine in short sentences
- Handle simple transactions (shop/cafe/directions)
