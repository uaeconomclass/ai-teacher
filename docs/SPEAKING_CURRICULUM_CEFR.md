# AI Teacher Speaking-First Curriculum (A1-C2)

## Goal
Build a full English learning path with strong focus on speaking, topic-based content, and clear grammar progression.

## Method
- Speaking-first: every lesson ends with live dialogue or role-play.
- Grammar-for-communication: grammar appears only as speaking tool.
- Topic-based progression: real-life themes for each CEFR level.
- Correction loop: error -> fixed phrase -> immediate reuse in speech.

## Lesson Standard (60 min)
1. Warm-up speaking (5 min)
2. Input: vocabulary + grammar pattern (10 min)
3. Controlled drills (15 min)
4. Role-play (15 min)
5. Free speaking task (10 min)
6. Feedback and retry (5 min)

## Weekly Standard (4 lessons)
1. New topic + phrase patterns
2. Grammar automation in speech
3. Scenario role-play
4. Fluency day (minimal interruption)

## Global Grammar Order (Communication Priority)
1. `to be`, pronouns, basic sentence order
2. Present Simple (affirmative/question/negative)
3. `there is/there are`, articles `a/an/the`
4. `can/can't` (ability/requests)
5. Past Simple
6. Future (`going to`, `will`)
7. Present Continuous
8. Comparatives/Superlatives
9. Present Perfect (experience/results)
10. Past Continuous + narrative links
11. First/Second Conditionals
12. Modals (`should`, `must`, `might`, `have to`)
13. Passive Voice (core use)
14. Reported Speech (core use)
15. Relative Clauses (`who/which/that`)
16. Advanced clauses, inversion, hedging, discourse control (C1-C2)

## Level Plan

### A1 (80-100 hours)
Goal: basic survival speaking in predictable contexts.

Core topics:
- Personal intro
- Family and friends
- Daily routine
- Food and shopping
- City and transport
- Weather and hobbies

Grammar focus:
- Items 1-4 + early Present Simple questions

Speaking outcomes:
- Self-introduction (1-2 min)
- Simple transactional dialogs (shop/cafe/directions)
- 30-60 second mini-monologues

### A2 (100-120 hours)
Goal: handle routine conversations in daily life.

Core topics:
- Travel and accommodation
- Health and appointments
- Work basics
- Home and services
- Plans and past events

Grammar focus:
- Items 5-8

Speaking outcomes:
- 5-7 minute conversation on familiar themes
- Tell a short story in the past
- Ask follow-up questions naturally

### B1 (140-180 hours)
Goal: communicate confidently in familiar social/professional contexts.

Core topics:
- Career and study
- Media and lifestyle
- Relationships
- Problems and solutions
- Culture and goals

Grammar focus:
- Items 9-12 (core)

Speaking outcomes:
- 8-10 minute structured conversation
- Give and defend opinions
- Explain choices and trade-offs

### B2 (180-220 hours)
Goal: spontaneous, sustained interaction with clear argumentation.

Core topics:
- Business communication
- Technology and society
- Environment
- Negotiation
- Education systems

Grammar focus:
- Items 12-15 with accuracy upgrade

Speaking outcomes:
- 12-15 minute discussion/debate
- Handle disagreement politely
- Clarify, reframe, and summarize arguments

### C1 (220-260 hours)
Goal: flexible, precise speaking in academic/professional settings.

Core topics:
- Leadership and ethics
- Economics and policy
- Innovation and research
- Intercultural communication
- Public speaking

Grammar focus:
- Advanced clause control, nuanced modality, discourse markers

Speaking outcomes:
- 5-7 minute presentation + Q&A
- Critical analysis with examples
- Register switching (formal/informal)

### C2 (260+ hours)
Goal: near-native fluency with nuance, precision, and rhetorical control.

Core topics:
- Complex social issues
- Specialized domains
- Rhetoric and persuasion
- Humor, irony, and tone control

Grammar focus:
- Fine-grained style control, advanced transformations, idiomatic range

Speaking outcomes:
- High-pressure spontaneous speaking
- Nuanced debate with precise framing
- Tone adaptation by audience/context

## Speaking Training System (Mandatory)
- `Shadowing` daily (2-4 min)
- `Repeat and improve` loop after feedback
- `3x retell`: short -> detailed -> opinionated
- Scenario cards: role, objective, constraints
- Error journal: personal top mistakes by level
- Spaced repetition for phrases, not isolated words

## Assessment Rubric (0-5 each)
- Fluency
- Accuracy
- Vocabulary range
- Pronunciation/clarity
- Interaction management

Passing rule:
- Minimum average 3.5/5
- No criterion below 3/5
- Successful completion of required scenarios

- B2: 12 topics

## Suggested Database Content Units
- 1 level = 8-12 topics
- 1 topic = 4-6 lessons
- 1 lesson = 5-8 exercises
- 1 exercise = prompt + expected patterns + scoring rubric

## Product Rules for AI Tutor
- Always prioritize conversation continuation over long theory.
- Correct max 2-3 high-impact errors per turn.
- Provide corrected phrase and force immediate reuse.
- Keep turns short at A1-A2, longer at B2+.
- Track recurring errors and recycle them in next sessions.
